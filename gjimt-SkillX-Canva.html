SkillX Canva Mastery Quiz

End-of-workshop quiz to check how confidently you can use Canva for real designs, social media content, and presentations. Your score will guide the right next SkillX workshop for you.

Student Details

How this quiz works

  • 10 questions are picked randomly from a pool of 30 Canva questions.
  • Mix of Easy and Medium questions focused on real Canva usage.
  • Each question has a 60-second timer; if time runs out, it auto-moves ahead.
  • Your performance band (Excellent, Good, Fair, or Repeat) will decide the next SkillX workshop suggestion.
This is not a rejection test. It helps the SkillX team understand how much you have benefitted from the Canva workshop and what support you need next.

What this quiz checks

The quiz is aligned with the SkillX Canva-Based Photo & Video Editing workshop and checks how confidently you can apply what you learnt on real tasks.

Using Templates Brand Kit & Consistency Resizing Designs Social Media Posts Slides & Presentations Photo/Video Editing Animations & Effects Export & Sharing

Outcome Bands & Next Step

  • 9–10 correct: Excellent – Canva Confident. Recommended: Presentation Confidence + advanced SkillX modules.
  • 7–8 correct: Good – Strong Canva Understanding. Recommended: Public Speaking & Presentation Confidence.
  • 5–6 correct: Fair – Needs Guided Practice. Recommended: Canva practice/revision batch.
  • 0–4 correct: Repeat / Support Recommended. Suggested: Repeat Canva SkillX / Digital Power Skills basics.
